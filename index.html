project

Real Time Automatic Poultry Farm Management

We four members in this project and my role in this project is to collect the informaton about the hardware equipeemnts and helping to making the project reports

Methodology

      The functionalities of our project is to handling the workloads of the
      poultry farmer such as  automatic  feeding food and water , the
      monitoring of capacities of feeds and storages, controlled overflowing in
      containers of water and feeds. Notification alerts for food capacities in
      feed storages and containers, gathering counts of the total chickens,
      controlling the temperature inside the poultry cage and also lives
      monitoring the activities of the chickens  inside the cage
